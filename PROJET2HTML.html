Appetizer

  • Loubia

    plat traditionnel réconfortant à base de haricots blancs mijotés dans une sauce tomate onctueuse et richement épicée.

    Harira

    Soupe marocaine traditionnelle à base de tomates, de lentilles, de pois chiches et de viande, parfumée aux épices et aux herbes fraîches.

    salade marocaine

    Salade marocaine colorée et rafraîchissante composée de tomates, de concombres, de poivrons, d'oignons rouges et d'olives, assaisonnée d'une vinaigrette à l'huile d'olive et au citron.

Main Courses

  • Tajine poulet aux olives

    Chicken cooked in a traditional Moroccan tagine with olives and aromatic spices.

    couscous

    Steamed semolina served with a flavorful stew of meat and vegetables, a staple of Moroccan cuisine.

    pastilla poulet

    A savory Moroccan pie made with layers of thin pastry, filled with a mixture of spiced meat chicken, almonds, and cinnamon, dusted with powdered sugar.

Desserts

  • Cornes de gazelle

    Delicate crescent-shaped pastries filled with a fragrant mixture of almond paste, orange blossom water, and cinnamon, dusted with powdered sugar.

    Ghriba

    Traditional Moroccan cookies made with semolina, sugar, and butter, often flavored with ingredients like almonds, coconut, or sesame seeds, resulting in a crumbly and delicious treat.

    Chebakia

    Sweet Moroccan pastries made from strips of dough, fried until crispy, and coated with honey or sugar.
